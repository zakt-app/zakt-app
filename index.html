About Zakt

At Zakt we believe the process of purchasing goods for your kids needs an upgrade! Our vision was born when we realized we need to write a check or get cash to give our kids to make purchases…the same way our parents did for us. We will not grant access to actual funds to your kids, but we will enable them to initiate a transaction digitally for you to decide to fulfill or not. The process is safe and secure and will modernize the way you parent.
